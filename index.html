PUGS STAR SYSTEM

Combined with powerful weapons, your pugs have the ability to take down Pug Bosses. Gain rewards as you climb the ladder in the Pug Mafia Metaverse. Pugs are classified through a star system that represent their power and number of weapons equippable. Limit of 10 pugs per crew.
